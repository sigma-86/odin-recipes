Fried Rice with Crab Meat

This is going to be Collin's favorite dish!


Ingredients

  • 2 tbls. olive oil
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1/4 cup shallots, diced
  • 1 tsp. Chinese five spice powder
  • 1/2 cup red peppers, diced
  • 3/4 cup diced ham
  • 1 cup frozen peas
  • 3/4 cup mango
  • 1 cup green onions
  • 2 tablspoons
  • 2 tablespoons sesame oil
  • 3 cups cooked rice
  • 8 oz. lump crab meat
  • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro

Steps

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large pan over a medium-high heat.
  2. Add the garlic, shallots and five rice powder and stir.
  3. Next add the peppers, ham, peas, mango, green onion and stir.
  4. Pour in the soy sauce and sesame oil and then add the cooked rice. Stir well.
  5. Finally add the crab meat and stir to combine. Cook until hot.
  6. Transfer the rice to a platter and garnish with lots of fresh cilantro. Serve at once.
