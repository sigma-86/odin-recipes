Dilled Seafood Salad Sandwiches

This is a refreshing change to the normal weekly meals.

Ingredients

  • 2/3 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 cup chopped cabbage
  • 1 sliced carrot (carrot sticks)
  • 1 tbls. dill
  • 1 tbls. lemon juice
  • 1/4 tsp. salt
  • 1/2 tsp. sugar
  • 1 garlic clove minced
  • 1/4 tsp. ground pepper
  • 8 oz. imitation crabmeat (flake style)
  • hamburger buns or hoagies

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix the first nine ingredients together.
  2. Next, break up the crab into bite size pieces and mix into the bowl.
  3. Serve on buns and your favorite side.
